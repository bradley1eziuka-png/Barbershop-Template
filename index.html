The Barber

One chair.
All focus.

Vine Street Barber is a one-person shop because that's how it should be. When you sit in the chair, you get the full attention of the person cutting your hair, not whoever happens to be free next.

Ten years cutting hair in Kalamazoo. All hair types, all styles: from tight skin fades to longer scissor cuts, from first-time customers to regulars who've been coming for years.

The shop is clean. The music is good. The conversation is optional. Show up, get a great cut, leave looking sharp. That's it.
